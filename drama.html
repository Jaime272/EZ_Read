Here are some Drama LIght Novels

Utsuro no Hako to Zero no Maria

Kazuki Hoshino cherishes his everyday life. Aya Otonashi is a new student and her arrival signifies a change in his lifestyle. "This is my 13,118th transfer. I'm here to break you". This is the first thing that was told to him. It is almost as mysterious and strange as her sudden appearance. The ordinary days will soon turn into that of confusion and disorder.

Log Horizon

ElderTale is a famous online multiplayer game. With the introduction of its 11th expansion. Almost 30,000 players found themselves whisked into the world of the game. Donning their avatars and confused, they realised that they were trapped in this world. Amongst them is Shiroe, a veteran player.

Durarara

In this rendition, Ikebukuro is a place where numerous oddities exist. There is a rumoured headless biker girl who silently rides along the highway. A bartender whose inhuman strength and power make all cower before him. Somewhere in the back alleys, there is a sadistic information broker. And lastly, Mikado Ryuugamine, a student who had just transferred to this odd place.

GoSick

In the small European town of Sauville, Kazuya Kujo is a student at a prestigious academy. His features and appearances earned him the nickname of the Black Reaper from his peers. However, he pays no heed to them and is only concerned with the supernatural. One day at the library, Kazuya chanced upon a mysterious doll-like looking girl. Victorique.

Welcome to the N.H.K.

Dropping out of college and deciding to live the life of a hikikomori, Tatsuhiro Sato has found a means to remove himself from society as best as he could. This lifestyle seems safe, as well as a good way to avoid the dreaded N.H.K., but soon Tatsuhiro will find fate has other plans for him.

Danganronpa/Zero

Before Danganronpa begins, here’s a story of an interesting young girl named Ryouko Otonashi. Ryouko is a fortunate student of Hope’s Peak Academy but unfortunately, her great school memories aren’t that great seeing as how Ryouko is a chronic amnesiac. While her friend Yasuke Matsuda tries to cure her disease, these two have a bleak future once an odd girl appears before them.
